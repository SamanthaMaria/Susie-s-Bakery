About Susie

Susie Sweeton was born and raised in central Wisconsin. She grew up helping her grandma bake cookies. This is where she fell in love with baking. From an early age Susie was always curious about creating different sweet treats. After graduating high school, she enrolled in pastry school with her dream of opening her own bakery.
